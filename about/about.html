Welcome to Our Login/Sign-Up Page!

Hey there! We’re glad you’re here. This application lets you easily create an account and log in for testing purposes.

What Can You Do Here?

  • Create a new account in just a few clicks!
  • Log in to access your personalized space.
  • Enjoy a simple and friendly interface.
  • Feel secure with password storage designed for your safety.
  • Switch effortlessly between login and sign-up forms.

Why This App?

This application was made to show you how a basic authentication system works using modern web technologies. It’s perfect for learning and getting familiar with the concepts of user authentication.

Technologies We Used

  • HTML: This forms the structure of our web pages.
  • CSS: This is what makes everything look great!
  • JavaScript: This handles user interactions and requests.
  • Node.js: This powers the backend for processing logins and sign-ups.

Getting Started is Easy!

To jump right in, click on the "Sign Up" link to create your account. Once you're signed up, logging in is a breeze!
